Chicken Tournedos

Image courteously from IGA

Description

Chicken Tournedos is a dish served with bacon wrapped chicken.
In the core of the Tournedo is melted cheese and mushrooms, with it being wrapped together in bacon.

Ingredients

  • 1 package (14 g) dried wild mushrooms
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • To taste, salt and pepper
  • 100 g (3½ oz.) finely sliced Oka (about half the cheese) or any other semi-firm cheese
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 4 wood skewers
  • Couscous
  • Green beans

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) or preheat barbecue to high.
  2. Place mushrooms in a small bowl of boiling water and soak for 10 to 15 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  3. Lay chicken thighs flat on a work surface. Cover in plastic wrap and use a meat tenderizer or skillet to flatten. Add salt and pepper.
  4. Cover chicken evenly with cheese and rehydrated mushrooms, then roll up chicken.
  5. Wrap each roll with a slice of bacon and use a skewer to hold it firmly in place.
  6. Fry chicken rolls in a large non-stick ovenproof skillet on high, then bake for 15 minutes or finish cooking on barbecue on medium for 10 to 12 minutes.
    The internal cooking temperature of the chicken must reach 77°C (170°F).
