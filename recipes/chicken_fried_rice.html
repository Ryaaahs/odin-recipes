Chicken Fried Rice

Description

Chicken Fried Rice is a dish that combines different hearty components to make filling meal.

Ingredients

  • 10 mL (2 tsp.) Compliments Canola Oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 stalk celery
  • 5 mL (1 tsp.) freshly grated ginger
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 red pepper
  • 250 mL (1 cup) sugar snap peas
  • 750 mL (3 cups) cooked rice
  • 250 mL (1 cup) cooked chicken
  • 15 mL (1 tbsp.) sodium-reduced soy sauce
  • 1 green onion, sliced diagonally
  • To taste, salt and pepper
  • 30 mL (2 tbsp.) toasted sliced almonds*

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Sauté the onion, celery, and ginger for 2 minutes.
    Add eggs and cook, stirring to scramble them. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  2. Sauté the pepper and sugar snap peas in the same skillet for 2 minutes. Add the chicken, rice, soy sauce, and reserved egg mixture.
    Mix well and continue cooking until well heated. Salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Garnish with green onion and grilled sliced almonds.

Info

*To toast the almonds, heat a small non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add nuts and stir gently for 1 minute or until fragrant and lightly browned.
